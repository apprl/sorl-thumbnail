ApparelRow is an online fashion startup based out of Stockholm Sweden.

ApparelRow aggregates items from a number of selected brands & retailers, and not only makes them easily searchable in one place, but also places those items in relevant contexts on apparelrow.com and on partner sites with fashion content. A key feature of ApparelRow is the close collaboration with leading bloggers & online fashion magazines, which creates contextual relevance to items.

ApparelRow lets users:

  • Browse & search items
  • See what items & looks have been most liked, commented and featured on blogs & sites etc.
  • Combine items in looks (collages or photos tagged with items) and share these looks either on apparelrow.com or other social networks or blogs.
  • Comment, discuss, rate and share items & looks on social platform of their choice.

ApparelRow is scheduled to launch during January 2011, and is now in the process of populating the site with relevant items from selected brands & retailers. The Swedish market will be the pilot market, and international roll-out is scheduled for late 2011.
